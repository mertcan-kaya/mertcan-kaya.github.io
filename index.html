About Me

Robotics Engineer and Doctoral Researcher specializing in motion planning, advanced control systems, and Human-Robot Interaction (HRI). My research explores how humans adapt their movements in shared spaces and how these principles can inform human-aware robot motion planning. I translate advanced control theory into fluid, deployable software for collaborative robots, with a proven track record of architecting trajectory generators, configuring autonomous mobile navigation loops, and deploying custom ROS/ROS 2 software stacks on commercial robotic platforms.
