Research Themes & Current Focus

Spatial Negotiation

Investigating how humans adapt their positions and spatial movement profiles when interacting in shared workspaces with robots.

Motor Interference

Investigating motor interference as an objective measure of perceived human-likeness in robot motion.

Human-Aware Motion Planning

Developing motion planning methods that adapt robot trajectories according to predicted human actions during collaborative tasks.

Hierarchical Planning

Formulating decoupled optimization targets to compute safe operational pathways without sacrificing tracking reactivity.

Inverse Optimal Control

Using inverse optimal control to infer task-relevant objectives from observed human movement trajectories.

Human Task Prediction

Employing stochastic modeling to infer human intentions and handle timing uncertainty in joint assembly tasks.
