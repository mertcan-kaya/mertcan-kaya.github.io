Research Vision

My research aims to enable robots to anticipate, understand, and adapt to human behavior during collaborative tasks. I investigate how humans perceive robot motion, how human actions can be predicted from observed behavior, and how these predictions can be integrated into motion planning and control systems to improve safety and operational efficiency.

Hardware Projects & Deployments

Direct empirical validation tracing the engineering stack from low-level embedded microcontrollers up to high-level system behaviors.

1. Autonomous Mobile Navigation & SLAM

Developed autonomous navigation systems using Nav2, Cartographer SLAM, and LiDAR-based perception on TurtleBot 4 and AgileX Scout Mini platforms.

  • Hardware: TurtleBot 4, AgileX Scout Mini, 2D/3D LiDAR, OAK-D.
  • Software: ROS 2 (Humble/Iron), Nav2 Framework, Cartographer SLAM, Python, C++.
  • Control: Behavior Trees (BT.CPP), EKF, AMCL.

2. Compliant Physical HRI & Force Control

Implemented hybrid position/force control loops for physical human-robot interaction. Ensured stable industrial trajectories while allowing safe kinetic adaptation during direct human contact or multi-agent load sharing.

  • Hardware: Stäubli RX160, Stäubli RX160L.
  • Software: Low Level Interface (LLI), C/C++.
  • Control: Impedance Control, Hybrid Position/Force Control, Inverse Kinematics.

3. Teleoperation & Predictive Human Tracking

Developed real-time teleoperation and predictive manipulator tracking loops. Tracked human spatial vectors actively using marker-based optical tracking to control manipulator behavior and evaluate motor interference.

  • Hardware: Universal Robots (UR3), OptiTrack Motion Capture System.
  • Software: Universal Robots RTDE, Python, C++.
  • Control: Marker Tracking, Trajectory Optimization, Intent Prediction.

4. Embedded Actuation & Firmware

Implemented embedded control architectures linking microcontrollers and ROS-based robotic systems. Validates full-stack robotics competence from device abstraction to high-level execution graphs.

  • Hardware: Raspberry Pi Pico, Multi-Axis Servos, Scissor-Lift.
  • Software: Micro-ROS, FreeRTOS, C/C++, UART/I2C/SPI.
  • Control: PID Motor Control, Kinematic Solvers, PWM.

Research Themes & Current Focus

Spatial Negotiation

Investigating how humans adapt their positions and spatial movement profiles when interacting in shared workspaces with robots.

Motor Interference

Investigating motor interference as an objective measure of perceived human-likeness in robot motion.

Human-Aware Motion Planning

Developing motion planning methods that adapt robot trajectories according to predicted human actions during collaborative tasks.

Hierarchical Planning

Formulating decoupled optimization targets to compute safe operational pathways without sacrificing tracking reactivity.

Inverse Optimal Control

Using inverse optimal control to infer task-relevant objectives from observed human movement trajectories.

Human Task Prediction

Employing stochastic modeling to infer human intentions and handle timing uncertainty in joint assembly tasks.
